North East India is one of the most popular tourist hubs in the country where you will find natural beauty in abundance. The pictures of the northeast seem to be unbelievable until you visit the places yourself. Here, you will find the perfect blend of lush greenery, deep forests, hills, and snow-capped mountain peaks. Besides, there are beautiful waterfalls and scenic valleys. The cherry on the cake is the way people welcome you to this region. They are generous, humble, and proud of their surroundings. The North-Eastern people in India know exactly how to conserve and protect their natural heritage for the generations to come. Boasting of mesmerising landscapes, exotic wildlife and destinations reflecting their culture and heritage, here are some of the best Northeast India destinations that you can visit during this summer.
